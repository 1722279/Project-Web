Submission guidelines
All paper submissions will be handled electronically.
Submitted papers must not substantially overlap with papers that have been published or that are simultaneously submitted to a journal or a conference with proceedings
Submitted papers will be peer reviewed and carefully evaluated based on originality, significance, technical soundness, and clarity of exposition
Information about the authors’ identities shouldn't appear anywhere in the text-body
Paper must be submitted in DOC format
The submitted papers must be between 4-6 pages
No Show Policy
Papers that are accepted must be presented at the conference, either by the authors themselves or via a proxy. In case a paper is not presented at the conference, it shall be deemed a ‘no-show’. Only presented papers will be submitted for indexing. No refund of the paid fees may be claimed by the ‘no-show’ author.
