Call for Papers

We would like to invite you to submit your papers to our International Conference on Information and Communication Technology for the Muslim World 2020 (ICT4M 2020) to be held in Kuantan, Pahang. Areas of interest include, but are not limited to:

Islamic Applications for Development of the Ummah
• IT in Quran and Hadith • IT in Arabic Language Processing • Evaluation of Islamic Applications • IT Ethics and Islamic Worldview • IT in Waqf Management • IT in Muslim NGOs and Human Rights • IT in Social Innovations • IT for Islamic Librarianship, Preservation and Archival • Islamic Application Models and Frameworks
Innovation in Islamic Finance
• Microfinance and Crowdfunding • Cryptocurrency and Distributed Ledgers • Digital and Mobile Payments • Digital Banking and Insurance • Digital Wealth Management • Regulation, Shariah Governance & Compliance • AI-based Fintech solutions • Fintech Data Analytics & Security • Threats & Risks management for Fintech
Computing Technologies and Applications
• Bioinformatics and Biosensors • Data Mining • Cloud Computing • Internet of Things • Computer Network and Security • HCI, Analytics and Brain-Computer Interface • Human-Centred Computing • Parallel and Distributed Computing • Algorithms and Control • Wireless Communication • Computer Vision • Business Intelligence and Analytics
Commercialization and Entrepreneurship of Innovative Research
• Co-Creation Innovation Model • Commercialization in Start-ups and Spinouts • Digital Transformation of Innovative Ecosystem • Persuasive Business Model • Best Practices and Challenges for Innovative Entrepreneurship
Information System Implementation, Governance and Challenges
• New Methodologies for IS Implementation • Digital Business Governance and Ecosystems • E-Government and E-Commerce • Social, Political and Social Implications of Digital Business • IS Adoption in Muslim Countries • IT and Education • Case Studies for Enterprise Systems • Issues in Emerging IT Applications
